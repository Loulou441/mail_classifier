Category rules:
- Problème technique informatique: any problem about a computer, a server, wifi connexion...,
- Demande administrative: Any HR request,
- Problème d'accès /Authentification: Any authentification problem, lost password...
- Demande de support utilisateur: Any help request,
- Bug ou dysfonctionnement d'un service: Any raised bug or malfunction, application crash...

Priority Rules:
- Critique: Emails mentioning urgent issues, deadlines, blocked processes, or a system crash...
- Élevé: Emails which raise concerning problems, with significant impacts, a high priority...
- Modéré: Emails which prompt a minor problem, with limited impact, a ponctual issue...
- Faible: Emails with simple questions, a non-urgent help request...
- Anodine: Emails with just a remarque, an observation...

Additional Rules:
- The summary must be 20 words maximum.
- Never invent information not present in the email.
- If unclear, choose the lowest reasonable priority.
